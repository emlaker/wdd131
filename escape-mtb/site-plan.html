Overview

Purpose

This site is all about connecting mountain biking fans. Whether you are sharing trail tips, your favorite places to ride, or just looking for cool people to ride with, we have a community page for you. Plus, there is a rental shop to hook you up with the gear you need to hit the trails. It is a one-stop spot for all things MTB!

Audience

My audience will be mountain bike enthusists and those passionate about the outdoors.

Dynamic elements

I will make a cool slideshow-style element that will cycle through photos automatically and if you click on the arrows you can cycle them manually as well. I will also make a submit form on the community page that will allow people to make comments on the page and ask questions. Maybe a pretend Credit Card like in the previous assignment with the ability to submit info.
